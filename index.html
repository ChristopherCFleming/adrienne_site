A Lifetime of Public Service

Adrienne is a dedicated advocate for equal access to local government, and a brighter future for all Cambridge residents and their families. Her personal journey, rooted in research and public service has shaped her vision for Cambridge. As a leader, Adrienne stands for inclusivity, affordable housing, and increased representation for marginalized communities. She envisions a Cambridge where resources are accessible to all, justice is equitable, and the environment is protected for future generations. By voting for Adrienne, you are voting for a candidate who will fight tirelessly to ensure a brighter future for families, promote social justice, and create a sustainable community. Join Adrienne in building a Cambridge that uplifts everyone.

Justice for All

Adrienne's approach to justice is rooted in the intersection of culture, health, and science, with a focus on access to justice for marginalized groups. As a Fulbright scholar in Argentina, she learned from the team who identified remains of victims of violence and worked tirelessly to challenge discrimination against female victims and their relatives who were written off as “crazy.” As a Technical Advisor for the National Network for Safe Communities, Adrienne spearheaded a focused deterrence program for lowering homicide rates. She then took these strategies to cities across the globe, emphasizing her dedication to creating safer communities.

A Better City
Starts at Home

Adrienne’s mother's deteriorating health prompted her to return to Massachusetts, where she has committed herself to supporting her mom while simultaneously engaging in public service. During this time, she married her Argentine husband, who she has supported in making the personal and professional transition to the United States. Together they navigated our complicated healthcare system, advocating for their son who underwent open-heart surgery at a tender age. These personal challenges have only fueled Adrienne's passion to ensure that Cambridge becomes a city that champions equal access to resources for all its residents.

Confronting COVID

At the height of the pandemic, Adrienne's dedication to public service and disaster response propelled her into action. When the already overburdened medical systems inevitably faltered, she helped forge a path foward. As a key member of the NYC Medical Reserve Corps, Adrienne's exceptional skills and experience came to the forefront as she took leave from her job to volunteer for the Fatality Management Task Force, ensuring critical civic responsibilities were accomplished amid the chaos. Adrienne's demonstrated commitment to crisis management and the well-being of others is a testament to the impactful leadership she will bring to Cambridge. Article
